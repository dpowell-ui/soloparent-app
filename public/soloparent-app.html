Step 4 of 5 · Community Agreement
The Society guidelines.

This is a safe space built on trust. These principles keep it that way for everyone.

🤝
Show up with honesty and grace.We're all at different stages. Speak your truth and extend the same kindness to others.
🔒
What's shared here, stays here.Stories shared in circles and discussions are held in confidence. This is a safe space — protect it.
🚫
No judgment, no unsolicited advice.We listen first. Advice is welcome only when asked for.
💬
Engage with intention.No spam, no self-promotion, no solicitation. Every interaction should add value to the community.
🌱
Give back when you're able.As you grow and heal, look for opportunities to encourage those who are a few steps behind you.
I have read and agree to the Solo Parent Society Community Guidelines. I understand that violations may result in removal from the community.
